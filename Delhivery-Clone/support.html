Shipment Status

‚àí How can I track my shipment?

To track your shipment, you can download our app or go to the tracking page. Entering your tracking ID (AWB/LRN) and click on 'TRACK'. You can also login using your mobile number to view all shipments being delivered to you by Delhivery and reach out to our customer support.

+ Why is my package delayed?

Package delays can occur due to various reasons including weather conditions, operational challenges, incorrect address details, or high shipment volumes during peak seasons. Please check your tracking status for specific updates about your shipment.

+ My shipment status is still at 'Pick up.' What should I do?

If your shipment status hasn't updated from 'Pick up' for more than 24 hours, please contact our customer support team with your tracking ID. They will investigate and provide you with an update on your shipment status.

+ What do I do if tracking page doesn't show the expected date of arrival?

If the expected delivery date is not displayed, it may be due to pending updates in our system. Please wait a few hours and check again. If the issue persists, contact our customer support for assistance.

+ My shipment is out for delivery. When will I receive it?

When your shipment is marked as 'Out for Delivery', it typically means you will receive it within the same business day. Our delivery partner will contact you before attempting delivery. Please ensure someone is available to receive the package.

+ How can I get the delivery of my shipment if I missed it today?

If you missed your delivery, you can reschedule it through our tracking page or mobile app. Alternatively, contact our customer support team who can arrange a re-delivery at your convenience.

+ Why can I see no update to the status of my shipment?

Lack of status updates can occur due to scanning delays at transit hubs or during the transportation process. Rest assured, your shipment is likely in transit. If there are no updates for more than 48 hours, please reach out to customer support.

+ How can I get the contact number of my delivery/pickup partner?

You can find the contact details of your delivery partner in the tracking page or our mobile app once your shipment is out for delivery. Alternatively, contact our customer support and they will provide you with the necessary contact information.

+ How can I reschedule delivery/pickup for my order?

You can reschedule your delivery or pickup through our tracking page by selecting a convenient date and time slot. You can also use our mobile app or contact customer support to arrange a reschedule.
